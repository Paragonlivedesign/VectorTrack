## VectorTrack 0.5.6 beta

### New and improved

- **Project Editor** — search, budget hours, assigned files list with unassign
- **Heatmap** — day detail panel + View in History
- **Project Summary** — smoother refresh without losing your selection
- Portable mode hidden from Settings (AppData only for installed builds)

### Install

Download **VectorTrack-0.5.6-Setup.exe** (desktop) and **VectorTrackScript_0.5.zip** (Vectorworks plug-in) from the assets below.

Full changelog: https://github.com/Paragonlivedesign/VectorTrack/blob/main/VectorTrack%200.5/CHANGELOG.md
